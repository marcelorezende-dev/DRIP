Sistema SIPRO

Maximising the tree-Caatinga in the rains, supplementing in the dry

What it is

SIPRO, also from Embrapa Semiárido, seeks maximum use of the tree-Caatinga during the rains, with dry-season supplementation of the herd from sorghum or maize stover, plus a buffel-grass area to support breeding females around lambing (Araújo Filho, 2013).

How it works

  • Graze the tree-Caatinga intensively in the rains
  • Supplement in the dry season with sorghum/maize stover (palhada)
  • Keep a buffel reserve for ewes/does at lambing and early lactation (Araújo Filho, 2013)
  • Add sorghum grain if lambing falls in the dry season (Araújo Filho, 2013)

Key facts

Core idea
max use of tree-Caatinga + targeted supplement
Supplement
sorghum / maize stover
Origin
Embrapa Semiárido
Best for
small ruminants
Note

A production system tuned to the breeding calendar, so females are best fed when it matters most.
