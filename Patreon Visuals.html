What is DRIP?

A living knowledge platform
for the world’s drylands.

DRIP gathers the research, frameworks, monitoring and stories of the Drylands Sustainable Landscapes Impact Program into one navigable home — mapped as a living network, organised under seven pillars, and kept current through an open archive.

Disclaimer

A DSL-IP project —
not a personal experiment.

DRIP is a project of the DSL-IP, funded by the GEF and the first engine of the DFSA. It is actively overseen by a UN Officer (programme design · complex programme management · AI · rural development) and relates to the behaviour-change assessments and investigations expected under the Programme’s research. Information is drawn from publicly available sources.

Grievance & data removal. If any information shown should be corrected or removed, a grievance mechanism is available on the platform — requests are reviewed and actioned promptly.
