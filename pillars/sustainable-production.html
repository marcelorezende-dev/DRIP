Sustainable Production

Producing food, fuel and income from drylands without degrading them. This pillar carries the Programme’s flagship delivery framework – the SLPF – and the modules through which it reaches farmers.

01

What this pillar holds

Sustainable Production is where assessment becomes action – the place the Programme turns evidence about a landscape into practices farmers can use.

It is anchored by the Sustainable Landscape Production Framework (SLPF), which combines three mutually reinforcing streams – Community Seed Banks (Vernooy et al., 2015), the Forest and Farm Facility (FAO & AgriCord, 2016) and Farmer Field Schools (FAO, 2016) – delivered as a coherent package rather than disconnected projects. Each stream works alone; their power is in integration (Waddington & White, 2014).
