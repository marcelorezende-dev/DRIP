Each card is a real WOCAT questionnaire, summarised and tagged the DRIP way. Open any card to read the full documentation on the WOCAT database. Classification follows the DRIP Typologies & Classifications (global WOCAT standard + DRIP conventions).

WOCAT is the World Overview of Conservation Approaches and Technologies. Data is a build-time snapshot; open each card for the live, authoritative record on WOCAT. A project of the DSL-IP.
