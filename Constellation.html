What it is

One system, many lenses

The Constellation maps the Drylands Sustainable Landscapes Impact Program as a network you can explore – drag to orbit, click a node to open it, and trace how coordination, knowledge and monitoring data move between the parts. It belongs to the platform's Coordination & Exchange pillar: the place where the whole Programme is aligned, pooled and connected.
